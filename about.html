Who we are

Zareen International Trading FZC is a dynamic UAE-based general trading company focused on supplying premium building materials, HVAC equipment and industrial lubricants. We supply contractors, distributors and retailers with reliable product sourcing, competitive pricing and end-to-end logistics support.

  • Bulk supply of construction materials — bricks, blocks, aggregates, plywood and more.
  • Wholesale HVAC & AC units, parts and commercial systems.
  • Industrial lubricants, greases and oils for automotive & industrial use.
  • Export & logistics support for regional and international clients.
