About Us

Zareen International Trading FZC is a UAE-based trading company specialising in bulk supply of construction materials, HVAC solutions and industrial lubricants. We combine deep supplier relationships, reliable logistics and local knowledge to deliver quality, on-time supply for projects and wholesalers across the Middle East.

What we do

  • Bulk supply of cement, hollow blocks, and construction goods
  • Wholesale HVAC units and spare parts
  • Industrial lubricants and private-label packaging
  • Fast logistics, local distribution and project support
